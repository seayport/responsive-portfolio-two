About Me

Sandy is a GA Tech employee, weekend runner and mentor to a 23 year old single Mom. She sells meeting space at the Global Learning Center in Midtown Atlanta. She has completed four marathons.

Last Fall, Sandy had the great fortune to go on an epic trip to South Africa and Zimbabwe. She had a wonderful time on safari and saw the Big Five. In Zimbabwe, she made fast friends with a homeless man named Moses. She also stretched beyond her comfort zone and went on a white water rafting trip down the Zambezi river. www.vicfallsrafting.com
